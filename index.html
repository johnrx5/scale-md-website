Our Clinical Staffing Solutions

Flexible, compliant, and high-quality physician networks designed to integrate seamlessly with your telehealth platform.

Asynchronous (Store-and-Forward) Telehealth

Perfect for direct-to-consumer (DTC) brands, our asynchronous solution allows patients to submit their information on their own time. Our physicians review and respond promptly, typically in under an hour, ensuring a convenient and efficient patient experience without the need for a live appointment.

  • Rapid, sub-hour response times to meet patient expectations.
  • Ideal for prescription refills, simple consultations, and follow-ups.
  • Reduces operational costs and increases physician efficiency.

Synchronous (Live Video) Telehealth

For services requiring real-time interaction, our network provides on-demand or scheduled video consultations. We staff your platform with board-certified physicians ready to deliver high-quality, personable care for urgent needs, initial diagnoses, and in-depth consultations.

  • 24/7/365 coverage for urgent care and immediate patient needs.
  • Specialist access for mental health, dermatology, and more.
  • Improves diagnostic accuracy and patient trust through face-to-face interaction.

Our Partnership Process

A streamlined, transparent process to launch and scale your clinical team.

Step 1: Discovery & Consultation

It begins with a conversation. We work closely with you to understand your clinical protocols, patient demographics, state coverage requirements, and desired service level agreements (SLAs). This ensures we build a solution perfectly tailored to your business model.

1
2

Step 2: Physician Matching & Credentialing

Using your specific criteria, our proprietary algorithm identifies the ideal physicians from our nationwide network. We then handle the entire credentialing process, verifying licenses, board certifications, and backgrounds to ensure compliance and quality before presenting you with a curated team for approval.

Step 3: Technical Onboarding & Training

We ensure your new clinical team is ready to go on day one. Our team manages the technical onboarding onto your EMR and telehealth platform, providing comprehensive training on your specific workflows, brand voice, and clinical guidelines to ensure a seamless patient experience.

4

Step 4: Launch, Monitor & Scale

Go live with confidence. We continuously monitor performance, response times, and quality metrics, providing you with transparent reports. As your patient volume grows, we dynamically adjust your physician staffing to meet demand, ensuring you can scale without compromising quality or compliance.
